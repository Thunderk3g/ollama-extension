Ollama Bridge Extension - Privacy Policy

Overview

The Ollama Bridge extension is designed to facilitate communication between websites and a local Ollama instance running on your machine. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information Collection and Usage

We do not collect any personal information or data from users. The extension operates entirely on your local device and serves only to facilitate communication between your browser and the Ollama API running locally on your computer.

The extension:

  • Intercepts specific API calls from websites to your local Ollama instance
  • Forwards these requests to your local machine (typically at http://localhost:11434)
  • Returns the responses back to the website
  • Stores minimal configuration settings in your browser's local storage (such as your preferred Ollama URL)

All data processing happens locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers or any third-party services.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • webRequest: To intercept and handle web requests to Ollama
  • storage: To save your settings and preferences
  • Host permissions: To access your local Ollama instance and specified websites

These permissions are used solely for the purpose of enabling communication between websites and your local Ollama instance.

Data Security

Since all data is processed locally and no personal information is collected, there are no data security concerns related to our extension. However, users should be aware that:

  • The extension facilitates communication between websites and your local AI models
  • You should only use the extension with websites you trust
  • The security of your local Ollama instance is your responsibility

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension.
